Popular Destinations

Discover the most beautiful places to visit in Jharkhand

Netarhat

Known as the "Queen of Chotanagpur," this hill station offers breathtaking sunrise and sunset views, dense forests, and a pleasant climate.

Explore

Dassam Falls

A magnificent waterfall on the Subarnarekha River, Dassam Falls is one of the most scenic spots in Jharkhand, especially during monsoon.

Explore

Betla National Park

Home to diverse wildlife including tigers, elephants, and leopards, this national park offers thrilling safari experiences amidst lush forests.

Explore

Rich Cultural Heritage

Experience the vibrant tribal culture and traditions of Jharkhand

Traditional Music

Experience the enchanting sounds of tribal instruments like mandar, nagara, and flute during cultural performances.

Festivals

Witness vibrant tribal festivals like Sarhul, Karma, and Sohrai that celebrate nature and harvest seasons.

Art & Craft

Admire the exquisite tribal art forms like Sohrai and Khovar paintings, and beautiful bamboo crafts.
